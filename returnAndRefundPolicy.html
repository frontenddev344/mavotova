Return and Refund Policy

At Mavotova (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "the website"), we strive to provide all our participants with an excellent user experience. Since our services are offered free of charge and we do not process financial transactions or sell products directly, no formal return or refund process is required.

1. No Financial Transactions

Our website does not process payments, financial transactions, or the sale of goods or services. All content and services provided by Mavotova are available free of charge, and therefore there are no purchases or financial obligations that would require refunds or returns.

2. Subscriptions and Premium Features

If your use of the website involves any form of subscriptions for services or premium features from third-party providers, such payments are processed by those external providers. In such cases, any issues related to refunds, cancellations, or returns must be resolved directly with the respective third-party provider in accordance with their policies and terms of service.

3. Account Termination

Should you no longer wish to use our services, you may close your account at any time. Since we do not process payments, there are no fees for terminating your membership or ending your participation in fantasy leagues or other activities offered on the website.
