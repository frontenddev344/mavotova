Privacy Policy

At Mavotova (hereinafter "we", "us" or "the Website"), we place great importance on the protection and respect of your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our Mavotova services (the "Service").

1. What information we collect

We collect personal data from you when you visit our website, create an account, participate in fantasy football leagues, conduct transactions, or interact with other users.

Your personal information may include:

  • Account Information: Name, username, email address, and password.

  • Profile Information: Team name, favorite teams, and league preferences.

  • Transaction Information: Payment details (for paid leagues or premium features), billing address, and other relevant information.

  • Interaction Data: Messages, comments, or other user-generated content.

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and activity logs on the website.

2. How we use your information

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide the service: Set up your account, manage your participation in fantasy football leagues, and ensure a seamless user experience.

  • Improve the service: Analyze trends and user behavior to enhance the functionality and features of the website.

  • Communicate with you: Send notifications, league updates, and announcements related to your participation or new features.

  • For marketing purposes: With your consent, send promotional emails about new features, offers, or products related to fantasy football.

  • Process payments: Facilitate secure transactions for paid leagues, subscriptions, or purchases.

  • Legal obligations: Comply with applicable legal requirements or enforce our terms of use.

3. We do not sell your data

Your privacy is important to us, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties under any circumstances. The information you provide is used solely to improve your experience on our platform, process your participation in fantasy leagues, and optimize the service overall. Any sharing of data only occurs in accordance with this policy and only with your consent or when required by law.

4. Sharing and disclosure of information

We respect your privacy and only share your personal information with third parties under the following circumstances:

  • With your consent: When you expressly agree to share your data with third parties.

  • Service providers: We may share your data with third-party providers who assist us in providing the service (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics providers), under strict confidentiality agreements.

  • Legal requirements: When we are required by law, regulation, or court order to disclose your information.

  • Business transfers: If we are subject to a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring company.

5. Data security

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. We use encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no online system can guarantee absolute security, so we cannot provide unlimited security.

6. Your rights and choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access and correction: You can access and update your personal data at any time by logging into your account.

  • Deletion of data: You can request the deletion of your personal data by contacting our support team, although certain data may be retained for legal or operational reasons.

  • Unsubscribe: You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time by following the instructions in the email or adjusting your preferences in your account settings.

7. Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. This helps us personalize your experience and analyze website traffic. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser or disable certain tracking technologies through your account settings.

8. Children's privacy

Our service is not directed at individuals under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us and we will promptly delete the information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
