How do I create an account?
To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button at the top right of the homepage. Enter your details, confirm your email address, and you can start your fantasy football journey.
How do I join a league?
After creating an account, navigate to the "Leagues" section. You can either join an existing public league or enter a code to join a private league created by your friends.
Can I make changes to my team during the season?
Yes, you can make changes to your team during the season. This includes setting your lineup, making trades, and picking up free agents from the waiver wire.
What happens if a player gets injured?
If a player on your team gets injured, you can replace them by picking up another player from the waiver wire or making a trade. Stay up to date with injury reports to make timely changes.
How do I stay updated on player news and stats?
Our platform provides real-time updates on player news and statistics. In addition, you can follow major sports news and use our integrated tools to stay informed.
Is there a mobile app?
Yes, our Mavotova platform has a mobile app for iOS and Android. You can download it from the App Store or Google Play.
How do I contact customer support?
If you need help, visit our "Contact" page and fill out the form or reach out to our support team by email at support@mavotova.com. We are here to help you!
